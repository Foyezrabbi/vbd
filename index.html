About Us

Volunteer for Bangladesh is a youth wing of JAAGO Foundation, established in 2011 with support of U.S Embassy to Bangladesh. Its goal is to empower young people in Bangladesh to volunteer and develop leadership skills to make positive change in their communities. It provides platform for community service, volunteer work, training, and mentorship opportunities.

What is Our Goal?

Our goal is to empower young minds to think critically and creatively about real-world problems and develop innovative, impactful solutions. Through Solution Hunt BD, we aim to:

  • Foster a culture of problem-solving<\b> & innovationamong<\b> students.
  • Encourage teamwork, leadership, and collaboration.
  • Provide a platform for participants to showcase their ideas to industry leaders & experts.
  • Inspire a new generation of changemakers who can address societal challenges effectively.

Event FAQs

How can someone participate in the competition?

To participate,

You need to register your team through the official registration form available on the website. Follow the instructions, submit the required information, and pay any applicable fees if required.

What is the maximum number of members allowed in a team?

A team can consist of a maximum of 5 members.

How should I dress ?
